About First

FIRST, For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology, is the organization that makes all this possible for us and thousands of other robotics teams. FIRST hosts 4 different robotics programs (ours being the FIRST Robotics Competition) that all have young people design and build robots to complete various tasks.
